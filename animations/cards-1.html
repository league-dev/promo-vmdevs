Meet Porcelain

Model 1.0

The Porcelain 1.0 AI model learns from text, images, and sensor data, adapting its parameters in real time. Its hybrid transformer–graph design enables both reasoning and creativity, making it ideal for dynamic, real-world decisions.

#01

Model 2.0

Porcelain 2.0 is an AI model built for language understanding and emotional context. It doesn’t just process words—it reads tone, intent, and nuance. Designed for digital communication tools, Porcelain 2.0 helps systems respond with empathy and precision, creating smoother, more human-like interactions between people and technology.

#02

Model 3.0

Porcelain 3.0 focuses on computer vision and spatial awareness. Trained on millions of real-world environments, it can detect, track, and interpret motion with remarkable accuracy. From autonomous drones to robotics, Porcelain 3.0 turns raw visuals into real-time understanding of the world.
