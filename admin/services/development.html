      Sharper QA, more conversions

      Here’s the deal: many websites lose up to 30% of their traffic because they don’t bother with proper testing. That means potential customers are bouncing because of things like broken layouts, slow load times, or buggy features. The result? Lost traffic, lower conversions, money down the drain.
      To give you an example, in the U.S., Chrome and Safari account for just 76% of desktop traffic. So what about the other 24% of users? They vanish into thin air. And if you don’t test your site on the other browsers (like Firefox, Edge, or Internet Explorer), you could end up with layout issues that ruin the entire user experience too. The same goes for different screen sizes, mobile devices, and various versions of the same browsers - Without thorough testing, all that traffic will just leave - and do business with your competitors.
      So, how do we stop the bleeding? We don’t just guess: it’s about rigorous and thorough testing via our QA process. We check, then check again,  and we do it whilst using all the tools at our disposal to ensure visitors get a smooth, consistent experience - no matter how they access your site. And in the end, that means more conversions and fewer headaches for you.
