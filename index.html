Projects supervised by a former designer

Developers don’t always catch what designers mean — and that’s how good designs end up built wrong, missing the small details that make them shine. At V&M, every project is guided by our co-founder, Vitalik Vinnikov, a former designer with five years of experience in UI/UX — making sure every nuance is captured right.
